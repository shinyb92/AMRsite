About Us

At Associated Material Resource we have three main goals: Save our customers money while providing the highest quality materials with unmatched service. As a supplier of raw plastic materials it is vital to succeed in all three areas. With experience since 1984 in engineering and commodity thermoplastics, as well as thermoset plastics, our customers benefit from our knowledge and expertise. Many things have changed over the last 30+ years there are new materials and processes that many couldn't have been imagined. What hasn't changed is (a) The need for plastics processors to increase profits by saving money (b) The need for high quality raw materials, free of contaminants, and (c) The need for timely delivery and technical support.
